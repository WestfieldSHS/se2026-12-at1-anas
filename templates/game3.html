Genre: Adventure / Mystery

Released: 2016

Developer: Campo Santo

⭐⭐⭐⭐

My personal Review:

Firewatch is one of those games that stays with you because of how effortlessly it blends beauty with unease. The scenery is honestly breathtaking — every sunset, every stretch of forest, every quiet moment in the watchtower feels like a painting you get to walk through. But what makes it special is how that peaceful world slowly shifts into something eerier, a mystery that creeps in at the edges and keeps you guessing without ever breaking the atmosphere. You’re constantly torn between admiring the view and wondering what’s hiding beneath it. The game pulls you in with its warmth, then surprises you with tension and uncertainty, all while never losing that sense of awe. It’s a story about isolation, connection, and the strange comfort of getting lost in a place that feels both safe and unsettling at the same time.
