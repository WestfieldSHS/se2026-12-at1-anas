My personal Review:

Five feet apart is a movie that will most likely make you cry. It's a movie about two people who have a condition called CP that doesn't allow them to touch each other. If they do touch they will likely die. Watching them fall in love is heartbreaking because we know its love that can't last. This movie is a beautiful presentation of love. The ending of this movie is a heart-breaking but necessary ending.
