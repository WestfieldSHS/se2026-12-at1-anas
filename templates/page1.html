Little Women

Duration: 2h 15m

Genre: Drama / Romance

Released: 2019

Cast: Saoirse Ronan

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

My personal Review:

Little Woman is an exceptionally made movie. This movie really leaves an impact on you with deep themes of loss and regret. The most memorable part of the movie for me was when Laurie (Timothée Chalamet) was confessing everything he felt to Jo. His acting made me feel everything as if I was standing on the sidelines. This movie will go down as one of my favorite romance movies of all time. Jo is the most idiotic person for not choosing Laurie.
