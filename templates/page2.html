Basketball Diaries

Duration: 1h 42m

Genre: Biography / Crime / Drama

Released: 1995

Cast: Leonardo DiCaprio

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

My personal Review:

Leonardo DiCaprio. That is the reason this movie was so beautifully portrayed. The acting of Leonardo was so spectacular. This movie is about a boy who is consumed by the harms of drugs. It shows that drugs will strip you of everything you hold dear. Relationships, education, sport. These are all things he lost because of stupid curiosity. The Basketball Diaries will personally go down as my favorite addiction movie.
