My personal Review:

Smallville is one of those shows that understands the weight of becoming someone you never asked to be. It captures Clark’s loneliness and the pressure of growing into a symbol the world expects, not the person he’s ready to become. What stood out to me was how the show treats heroism as something painful, something earned through identity and sacrifice. It’s a story about surviving yourself more than saving anyone else. Apart from all that, Lex switch was crazy when i first saw it.
