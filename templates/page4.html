My personal Review:

The greatest end to a trilogy, even possibly better than the ending to The Lord of the Rings. The emotions that were conveyed were phenomenally displayed. This movie really showed the impact of discrimination that the apes had to deal with. Wholeheartedly, I believe that any creature with the ability to present emotions must be treated with the same values as humans. Deserves a 5 because of how intense I felt through the movie.
