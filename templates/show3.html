My personal Review:

Game of Thrones is one of those shows that stays incredible no matter how you feel about the ending. The world-building, the tension, and the character arcs are so strong that the journey overshadows the destination. Jon Snow carries the show with this quiet strength, Jaime Lannister has one of the coolest redemption arcs ever put on TV, and Tyrion steals every scene with how sharp and human he is. What I took from the show was that power exposes people more than it changes them, and watching that unfold was unforgettable..
