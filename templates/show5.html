My personal Review:

Gossip Girl is a pretty enjoyable show, the kind of drama that keeps you watching because of how chaotic and entertaining everyone’s lives are. It’s fun seeing these characters treat every secret like a sport, and the show never takes itself too seriously, which makes it even better to watch. My favorite moment will always be “Three words. Eight letters. Say it and I’m yours,” a scene that somehow manages to be dramatic, iconic, and unintentionally hilarious at the same time.
