My personal Review:

Avatar: The Last Airbender is one of those shows that hits you harder the older you get. It’s a story about identity and balance, showing how Aang isn’t just learning the elements, he’s learning how to carry the weight of a world that expects everything from him. What stood out to me was how every character is fighting their own internal battle, especially Zuko, whose entire arc feels like a lesson in breaking cycles. The show reminds you that real strength isn’t power, it’s understanding. Apart from all that, Appa was carrying the whole team like it was light work.
